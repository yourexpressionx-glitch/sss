Our Journey

15+ years of building trust and excellence in the security industry.

2008 - Humble Beginnings

Founded in Siliguri with a small team and bold vision to transform security services in North Bengal. Started with 20 trained personnel serving 5 local clients.

2012 - Regional Expansion

Expanded operations to Darjeeling, Kalimpong, and Cooch Behar. Launched facility management services. Personnel strength grew to 150+. Achieved 100% statutory compliance certification.

2015 - Armed Security Division

Established licensed armed security division. Trained black commando and special response units. Expanded into Sikkim region. Client base reached 30+ major accounts.

2018 - Digital Transformation

Implemented centralized digital management system for real-time monitoring and reporting. Launched dedicated HR and training academy. Personnel strength exceeded 350+.

2021 - Multi-Sector Dominance

Serving 50+ client locations across manufacturing, banking, healthcare, hospitality, and government sectors. Female security division established. PAN India expansion initiated.

2024 - Industry Leader

450+ trained and certified personnel. Multi-location deployment capability (20-1000+ guards). Industry recognition for compliance excellence and client satisfaction. Expanding PAN India presence.
