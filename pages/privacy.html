Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

Siliguri Security Services Pvt. Ltd. ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website and use our services.

  • Contact information (name, email, phone number)

  • Service requirements and preferences
  • Payment and billing information
  • Any other information you voluntarily provide

2.2 Automatically Collected Information
