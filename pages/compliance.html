Background Verification Process

100% verification for every personnel hired by Siliguri Security Services.

Step 1: Personal Document Verification

  • Aadhar card verification
  • PAN card validation
  • Passport/ID proof check
  • Address proof verification
  • Contact information validation

Step 2: Criminal Record Check

  • Police verification from registered address
  • Previous locality verification
  • Criminal background clearance
  • No criminal conviction
  • FIR database cross-reference

Step 3: Character Reference Verification

  • Previous employer reference check
  • Community/local reference verification
  • Neighbor/landlord assessment
  • Educational institution verification
  • Professional conduct assessment

Step 4: Medical & Physical Fitness

  • Medical examination by certified doctor
  • Fitness certification
  • Age & physical capability assessment
  • Health condition declaration
  • Annual medical certification renewal

Step 5: Psychological Assessment (For Armed Personnel)

  • Psychological evaluation by psychologist
  • Mental fitness certification
  • Emotional stability assessment
  • Behavioral evaluation
  • Annual re-assessment requirement

Step 6: Drug & Substance Testing

  • Pre-employment drug test
  • Random substance screening
  • Alcohol testing protocols
  • No history of substance abuse
  • Regular testing for armed personnel
